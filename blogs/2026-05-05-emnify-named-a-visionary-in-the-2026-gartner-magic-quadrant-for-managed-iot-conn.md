Recognized for Completeness of Vision and Ability to Execute We are excited to share that emnify has been recognized a Visionary in the 2026 Gartner® Magic Quadrant™ for Managed IoT Connectivity Services. At emnify, our perspective is simple: managed IoT connectivity should help enterprises deploy faster and stay in control as IoT projects become more complex. Modern technology teams need to get devices online quickly, keep operations visible, and make connectivity decisions without waiting on manual provider processes. This is the role we built the SuperNetwork to play: a cloud-native global IoT connectivity platform that helps enterprises manage mobile connectivity across 550+ networks in 195+ countries and territories from one place. For use cases such as fleet management, EV charging, asset tracking, point of sale systems, aviation solutions, digital signage and connected consumer devices, that means faster rollout, resilient coverage, secure routing, usage control, real-time visibility and the ability to act without waiting on manual provider processes.
