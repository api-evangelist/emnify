Berlin, 28 January, 2026 - emnify, a global leader in IoT connectivity, has been named Gold Winner in the Telecoms Cloud Innovation category at the Juniper Research Future Digital Awards for Telco Innovation 2026 . The award recognises emnify’s cloud-native mobile core network, which gives enterprises unmatched speed and control over global connectivity.
