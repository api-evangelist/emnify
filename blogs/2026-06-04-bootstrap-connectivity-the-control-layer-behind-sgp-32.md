Bootstrap connectivity serves as the foundational control layer enabling SGP.32 remote SIM provisioning in IoT deployments, supporting fallback, recovery, and diagnostics throughout a device's operational lifetime. Rather than becoming less important with SGP.32's flexibility, bootstrap connectivity becomes strategically critical for managing connectivity across global IoT fleets that may remain active for years.
