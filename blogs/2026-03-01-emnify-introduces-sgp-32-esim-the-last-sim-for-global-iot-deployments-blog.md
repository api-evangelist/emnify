Barcelona, March 2, 2026 – IoT connectivity is shifting from physical SIM logistics to software-driven control. With the GSMA’s SGP.32 standard , connectivity can now be managed programmatically across the full device lifecycle - from factory provisioning to in-field optimization. emnify, a global leader in cloud-native IoT connectivity, is introducing the new SGP.32 eSIM standard at scale through its IoT SuperNetwork and operating model. Customers can leverage the new capabilities to deliver three outcomes: instant-on connectivity through factory-ready bootstrap provisioning, true provider independence across a device’s lifetime, and built-in structural resilience through secure fallback options.
