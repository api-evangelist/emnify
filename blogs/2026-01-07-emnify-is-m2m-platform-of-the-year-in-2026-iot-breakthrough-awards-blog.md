BERLIN, Jan. 7, 2026 – emnify , a global leader in IoT connectivity, today announced that it has been selected as winner of the “M2M Platform of the Year” award in the 10 th annual IoT Breakthrough Awards program conducted by IoT Breakthrough , a leading market intelligence organization that recognizes the most innovative companies in the global Internet-of-Things (IoT) market today.
